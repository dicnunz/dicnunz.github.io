Boundary

What this cannot do

This sentinel does not send, schedule, address, or authorize external contact. It makes stale proof unusable and routes the next local branch to renewal work.

It does not claim target-company affiliation, endorsement, employment, customer deployment, revenue, incorporation, funding, legal status, trademark ownership, or production results.

Invariant

Rules that do not move

  • No outbound before 2026-05-26 unless a verified real reply appears or Nic gives a fresh same-thread override.
  • No legal, tax, banking, payment, trademark, contract, hiring, fundraising, customer, revenue, or incorporation action is authorized by this map.
  • No affiliation, endorsement, employment, customer deployment, revenue, funding, official status, or target-company traction is claimed.
  • The application receipt is delivery evidence only; it is not an interview, offer, collaboration path, recruiter-owned next step, or human reply.
  • Every claim promotion needs a file, command, public URL, ledger receipt, graph node, source note, or explicit blocker.
  • Benchmark patterns may become CIW-owned mechanisms; benchmark brand, trade dress, screenshots, customer logos, partner badges, and official-status claims may not.
  • Public surfaces must remain private-data-clean and non-confusing before they are used as leverage.

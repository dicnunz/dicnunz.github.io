Four launch bands, one public-safe control surface.

This page separates what can be shown now, what can keep checking itself, what may support an OpenAI-role review after the date gate, and what is explicitly not ready for company launch.

Hard gates

Before any launch signal

Public proof launch

Ready

The public packet can launch as an inspectable proof route: links first, receipts second, no claims beyond what the pages and repos show.

  • Primary route: neutral role-review page into digest, ledger, outcome watch, and source.
  • Use case: recruiter, reviewer, or collaborator can inspect without needing private context.
  • Limit: this is proof publication, not outreach permission.

Proof lab

Autonomous proof-lab launch

Audit-only

The lab can keep producing public-safe receipts and status checks, but the loop stays audit-only until the current no-outbound gate expires or the route receives a verified trigger.

  • Healthy surfaces: ledger inspector, autonomy status, regression suite, and staleness sentinel.
  • Allowed work: refresh checks, source mapping, route repair, proof graph updates, and verifier receipts.
  • Blocked work: external messaging, commercial commitments, or target-company outcome assumptions.

Hiring route

OpenAI hiring leverage

Conditional

The proof web may support an OpenAI-role review only after the route passes freshness, source, draft-quality, and no-outbound checks. Until then, it remains a local/public proof packet.

  • Strongest angle: Codex workflow adoption evidence, verifier thinking, field kits, and handoff artifacts.
  • Required before use: fresh role source, fresh outcome watch, no-send gate pass, and safe recipient fit.
  • Boundary: application receipt is not outcome evidence, and this page does not imply OpenAI response.

Company line

Legal/commercial company launch

Not ready

Cleanroom Intelligence Works is not launched here as a legal or commercial company. This band is a stop sign, not a quiet go-ahead.

  • Allowed now: keep the concept public-safe, source-backed, and clear about boundaries.
  • Required later: explicit fresh authorization plus qualified review before formation, naming, accounting, transaction rails, staffing, capital, or binding terms.
  • Current public posture: proof lab and role-review packet only.

Release rule

How to read this page

  • Green means a public proof surface can be linked without pretending it is an outcome.
  • Audit-only means the system can verify and improve itself, not contact people.
  • Conditional means the route needs a fresh gate pass before it can become hiring leverage.
  • Not ready means do not compress proof-lab credibility into company-launch theater.

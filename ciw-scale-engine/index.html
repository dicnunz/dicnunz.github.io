Agents

Ownerless work lanes

Strategy Selector Heartbeat finds no required failure and the growth plan exposes a non-redundant safe branch. Growth plan JSON, graph node, and passing verifier. No outbound and no legal/commercial move; repair failed gates before strategy.
Builder Lane Selected branch needs code, page, fixture, or data-model work. Unit tests, render command, and diff-limited file list. No outbound and no legal/commercial move; stop on unowned destructive edits.
Verifier Lane Any artifact is promoted from note to proof. Failing fixture for unsafe cases and passing fixture for valid cases. No outbound and no legal/commercial move; refuse proof without a runnable check.
Source Lane Benchmark, role, policy, platform, or competitor facts influence public strategy. Source-backed memo with date, URL, and claim boundary. No outbound and no legal/commercial move; do not use stale or unsourced claims.
Public Surface Lane Graph, ledger, route index, digest, or page content changes. QA pass plus public marker pass or exact deploy blocker. No outbound and no legal/commercial move; do not publish private paths or confusing marks.
Safety Lane A copied pattern, benchmark claim, growth claim, or protected mark appears. Banned-claim scan, privacy scan, and claim-boundary note. No outbound and no legal/commercial move; do not let ambition override source truth.

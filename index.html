How To Read This

What I am trying to prove.

AI-assisted, clearly labeled

I use AI heavily for coding and iteration. The work is presented that way on purpose, with emphasis on judgment, verification, and the finished artifact.

Runnability matters

The strongest projects include clear setup paths, sample outputs, screenshots, tests, reports, or live pages so the work can be checked quickly.

Product shape matters

I care about narrowing the idea, making the workflow obvious, and turning technical experiments into something another person can understand fast.

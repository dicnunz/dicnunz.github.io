Why This GitHub Works

The portfolio is intentionally opinionated.

Local-first by default

Most repos are usable without a SaaS backend or paid API, which makes them easier to trust, rerun, and inspect.

Proof-heavy presentation

Screenshots, reports, demos, and reproducible commands do more work here than vague benchmark claims or abstract pitch copy.

Technical taste over volume

The point is not to show every experiment. The point is to show the repos that best communicate how I think and build.

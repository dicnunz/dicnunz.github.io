Role fit

Why these links

  • Demos and reference implementations: receipt validator and fixtures.
  • Outcome state: read-only watch shows application receipt only and forces refresh on May 26, 2026.
  • Application delta: post-receipt proof changes are visible without turning the receipt into target-side traction.
  • Completion discipline: objective audit refuses to call proof-lab readiness the same thing as OpenAI-side outcome evidence.
  • Regression gate: single-pass route-source, staleness, renewal, draft-quality, route-index, ledger, public-URL, privacy, and no-outbound checks.
  • Security review cadence: data boundaries, secrets, tool permissions, human approval, audit receipts, rollback containment, and security communication before proof promotion.
  • Cookbook-style example: public-safe recipe packet with commands, expected markers, role-signal coverage, and extension gates.
  • Stale-route refusal: staleness sentinel rejects stale role, digest, route-index, ledger, graph, or no-outbound state.
  • Stale-route repair: renewal drill turns refusal into local source, digest, route-index, ledger, graph, autonomy, and gate repair.
  • Follow-up judgment: no-send kit with send, hold, and do-not-send rules for post-gate decisions.
  • Draft judgment: follow-up draft evaluator scoring freshness, fit, new proof, claim safety, repetition, link economy, and date-gate compliance.
  • Workshops and enablement: repair-to-ready post-workshop handoff lineage.
  • Technical content: field kit, patterns guide, proof brief, and digest.
  • Product feedback: feedback packet renderer, simulated feedback export, and filled sample run.
  • Field-trial acceptance: scorecard fixture with CI-backed role-need coverage and release gates.
  • Interview conversion: assessment drill fixture with CI-backed engineering and deployment signal coverage.
  • Cold-read traversal: simulation fixture that checks whether a reviewer can recover the route inside a fixed time budget.
  • Source lookup: source-to-answer index with exact primary and supporting proof links.
  • Objection handling: branching drill with source-backed answers for tenure, technical depth, enablement, product judgment, safety, adoption readiness, and next-step questions.
  • Reviewer comprehension: time-to-understand benchmark with route budget, source verifiability, and boundary checks.
  • Operational readiness: public proof ledger, CI, scheduler health, autonomy heartbeat, and scorecard-gated next-work packet.
